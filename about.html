Hi, I’m Sandy. I hold a BFA Honours in Visual Arts and Philosophy from York University and an MA in Contemporary Art Theory from Goldsmiths College, University of London.

I make art and write things. All of my work is informed by my research interests, which changes all the time. Right now, they includes subversive modes of temporality, the effects of digital immortality on labour and time, Marxist-feminist concepts of work, sea creatures from the Cambrian, iridescence and probably a lot of other topics.

Oh, and I also design and make websites. Nothing super complicated (yet), mind, but I made this one so ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
