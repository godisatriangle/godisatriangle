Aberdeen: Art and Culture in the Granite City

Originally published on ArtRabbit in 2019.

The bid to make Aberdeen more culturally engaging is a practical move by tourism boards to relieve some of Edinburgh’s tourist congestion. By redirecting the streams of visitors further north, there’s hope that individuals will discover the cultural offerings of Scotland as not simply isolated to the capital. In this vein, Aberdeen is making a name for itself as a cultural staple for championing street and contemporary art, design, jazz and more.
